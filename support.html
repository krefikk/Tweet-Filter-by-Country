How to Use?

Turn on the extension and open the X main page (timeline). It will start hiding tweets from locations you don't want to see.

Features

Smart Speed (Anti-Ban)

Simulates human reading speed (3-5 seconds delay) when checking a user for the first time to prevent your account from getting locked. Once a user is identified, they are cached locally and future blocks happen instantly (0ms).

Hiding Modes

  • Standard: Tweets are just hidden. You can not reveal them anymore.
  • Standard with Placeholder: Tweets are hidden with a "Hidden" placeholder. You can click to reveal.
  • Auto-Mute (Orange): Hides the tweet and automatically mutes the user.
  • Auto-Block (Red): Hides the tweet and automatically blocks the user. Use with caution!

System Lock (Red Alert)

If X (Twitter) limits your request rate for sending too much location request (should not be happen, contact me if it happens), the extension will automatically lock itself for 15 minutes to protect your account. You will see a countdown on the settings page.
